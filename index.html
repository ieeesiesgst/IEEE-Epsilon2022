ABOUT EPSILON 2022

Epsilon is an Academic Convention wherein the invited Industry Experts will speak about their expertise in the field of Internet Spectrum.This time, we have got covered security over various domains like websites, 5G and IOT, everything from Blockchains to cybersecurity and many more. Our target is to get around this vast domain starting right from the genesis and pan it up to the conceptual level.


TRACKS FOR EPSILON

IEEE Track

Imagine a world where you can send money directly to someone without a bank – in seconds instead of days, and you don’t pay exorbitant bank fees. "Blockchain" is an unchangeable distributed digital record of transactions or data stored in multiple places on a computer network, with many use cases beyond cryptocurrencies. In this track, we'll learn about Blockchain as an emerging technology and the problems it faces, as well as practise secure code and get hands-on with smart contracts.

Computer Society Track

Cybersecurity refers to the safeguarding of internet-connected devices and services against malicious attacks by hackers, spammers, and cybercriminals. A continuous growth in cybercrime exposes weaknesses in the gadgets and services we've grown to rely on. We've got you covered on everything under the CS Track of Epsilon 2022, from cybersecurity insights to various cybersecurity issues in diverse areas. We will also learn about current cybercrimes that have had a significant impact on numerous organisations, among other things.

MTT-S Track

Our smartphones, smart watches, and other devices are how we connect to the internet securely. These devices greatly simplify our daily life. But have you ever thought about how much our lives might be made simpler if we connected more devices to the internet? A recent technological advancement is the Internet of Things, and it has arrived, but there is also the matter of safety and security. The ultra-fast 5G network will increase the interconnectedness of the Internet of Things (IoT) and allow for more effective data transport. Let's explore the 5G and IoT worlds.

FAQs

What is Epsilon 2022?

Epsilon is a three day academic convention organized under IEEE SIESGST from 13th to the 15th of August, 2022.

Is there any registration fee?

It is completely free for everyone. All it takes is your interest and time

Why should I participate?

Epsilon provides a great opportunity to learn the selected topics all the way from the beginner to expert level. Participants will receive certificates and best performers will receive prizes!

Who can participate for Epsilon 2022?

Anyone who is inclined towards learning new emerging technologies.
